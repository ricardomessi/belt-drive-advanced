Engineering Calculations

Hub bearing force at each pulley. Computed from tangent geometry and span tensions.
Fx = Tin·cos θin + Tout·cos θout
Fhub = √(Fx² + Fy²)
θhub = atan2(Fy, Fx) × 180/π
Tight-side, slack-side, centrifugal and total dynamic tensions per span.
Tc = mb · v²mb=0.18 kg/m · 8-rib Aramid
Ttight = Tstatic + Teff/2
Tslack = Tstatic − Teff/2
Ttotal = Ttight + Tc
Capstan equation slip safety factor per pulley. SF < 1 = belt slipping.
Ttight/Tslack ≤ eμθμ=0.35 rubber-on-steel
SF = ln(Ttight/Tslack) / (μ·θ)SF > 1.3 → safe zone
Wöhler fatigue model with Palmgren-Miner damage accumulation per duty-cycle weights.
Nf = Nref · (Tref/Ttight)mm=10, Nref=108, Tref=1200 N
D = Σ(wi / Nfi)Palmgren-Miner rule
Life = Lbelt / DLbelt=1.577 m
ISO 281 L10 bearing life for water pump shaft bearings (C&U data). WP driven at 1.35× engine RPM via geartrain.
L10 = (C/P)p × 106/(60·n)Ball: p=3 · Roller: p=10/3
L10,composite = [Σ(L10,i−9/8)]−8/9
Extra frictional power from engaging the A/C compressor vs non-AC baseline.
2.1 kW
ΔTspan = ΔPAC / vbelt
